History of the Tunnels

The Shanghai tunnels are said to be one of the most haunted places in America. Shanghaiing was a common practice in the city where people where used as slaves to perform various types of work. The area was originally for businesses such as bars and restaurants. The tunnels were connected to the basements of these structures to acquire the supplies that came in. Trap door systems were soon developed by the individuals who practiced Shanghaiing. Drugs or alcohol was used to subdue men and women so that by the time they woke up, it would be too late to go back. It is believed that nearly two thousand individuals were brought into the tunnels and there have been reports of paranormal activity as a result of the emotional energy that took place.

Visiting the tunnels

There are guided tours available if you are interested in exploring these haunted tunnels. These tunnels share a scary history exposing the dark past of Portland.
